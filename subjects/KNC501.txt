5.25. What do you mean by e-governance ?
Ans. The electronic governance or e-governance implies, government
functioning with the application of ICT (Information and
Communications Technology).
5.26. What are the types of e-governance ?
Ans. There are 4 types of e-governance :
1. G2C (Government to citizens)
2. G2B (Government to business)
3. G2E (Government to employee)
4. G2G (Government to government)
5.27. What do you understand by the term alienation ?
Ans. Alienation is a theoretical concept developed by Karl Marx that of
describes the isolating, dehumanizing, and disenchanting effects
working within a capitalist system of production.
5.28. What do you understand by the term secession ?
Ans. Secession is the withdrawal of a group from a larger entity, especially
a political entity, but also from any organization, union or military
alliance.
